This story

Why OSHYN exists

OSHYN was created by the one and only NEHA who spent years watching good businesses fail at digital marketing — not because they lacked the budget, but because they were sold tactics instead of strategy, vanity metrics instead of outcomes, and complexity instead of clarity.

We built OSHYN to be different: an agency where every engagement starts with "what does your business actually need?" — and where we measure our own success by your results, not your retainer.

We're based in Vancouver because we believe in knowing the market you serve. Metro Vancouver is competitive, specific, and constantly evolving. Our local expertise isn't just a tagline — it's baked into every strategy we build.

"Marketing shouldn't be a mystery. It should be a system — built on data, tested relentlessly, and optimised until it works. That's the OSHYN philosophy."

Clarity over complexity

We explain what we're doing, why, and what you should expect — in plain language.

Results over reports

We don't hide behind vanity metrics. If it's not generating revenue, we change the approach.

Strategy before execution

The right direction matters more than moving fast. We build the map before we start driving.

Long-term thinking

We build assets — rankings, audiences, content — that compound over time instead of disappearing.

We work best with:

  • BC businesses generating revenue who want to scale
  • Founders who value strategy and want to understand the "why"
  • Teams who want a real partner, not just a vendor
