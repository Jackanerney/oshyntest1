Working with OSHYN

We work best with established small to mid-size businesses in Metro Vancouver and BC — professional services, health & wellness, home improvement, legal, real estate, hospitality, and retail. If you're generating revenue and want to grow your digital presence, there's a strong chance we can help.

Three things stand out: First, we're strategy-first — we won't execute tactics without understanding your business goal. Second, we report on revenue impact, not vanity metrics. Third, your account is managed by senior strategists, not junior coordinators. We're also a smaller team which means you get real attention, not a case number.

Once you approve the direction, we build a 90-day roadmap with clear KPIs, and campaigns launch from there. You're kept informed at every step.

Pricing & Budget

Our ongoing retainers start at approximately $1,500/month depending on the service mix. Paid advertising management is typically priced as a percentage of ad spend. Strategy engagements and audits are available as one-time projects starting from $1,200. We'll always give you a clear proposal after the discovery call — no surprises.

We recommend a minimum of $1,000/month in ad spend for Google or Meta campaigns to gather meaningful data and optimise effectively. Below that threshold, it's difficult to generate statistically significant results. We'll always be honest if your budget isn't enough to achieve your goals.

Results & Timelines

It depends on the service. Paid advertising campaigns can generate leads within 2–4 weeks of launch. SEO typically shows meaningful traction at 3–6 months — it builds over time and compounds. Social media engagement improvements are usually visible within 4–8 weeks. We set honest timelines upfront and track progress from day one.

What we guarantee is a rigorous, transparent process, experienced strategists working on your account, and monthly reports that tell you exactly what's working and what needs to change.
