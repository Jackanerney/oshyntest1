The challenge

12 years in business, stellar reputation, but ranking on page 3+ for every relevant keyword. Website was outdated, had no blog, poor technical health, and had never had any SEO investment. Competitors with less experience ranked above them.

The challenge

Our approach

  • Brand voice audit and messaging framework
  • Platform-specific content strategy (Instagram + Facebook)

Paid Advertising + CRO

Harbour Legal Group

A Vancouver law firm running Google Ads in-house with a significant monthly budget but a cost per lead that made the channel unprofitable. They were getting clicks but not conversions — and didn't know why.

-52%
Cost per qualified lead
2.8×
Conversion rate lift
$4.20
Revenue per $1 spent
90d
To break even & grow

The challenge

Campaigns were broad, bidding on generic terms, sending traffic to the homepage. No conversion tracking. No A/B testing. No understanding of which keywords actually brought in paying clients vs. time-wasters.

Our approach

  • Full Google Ads account audit — restructured 3 campaigns
  • Negative keyword list — eliminated irrelevant traffic
  • New landing pages for each practice area
  • Conversion tracking setup and call tracking
  • A/B testing headlines and CTAs
  • Monthly optimisation cycle with budget reallocation

The outcome

Within 90 days, cost per qualified lead dropped 52%. ROAS went from 0.9× (unprofitable) to 4.2×. The same budget was now generating more than 4× the revenue. The firm expanded their budget by 60% in month 4.
